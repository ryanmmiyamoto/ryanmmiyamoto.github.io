Overview

In July 2019, I was contacted by a dental office in Newport Beach, CA to design and develop a website that would serve as their primary marketing asset. After assessing their needs and taking their budget into consideration, I determined that WordPress would be the most functionally appropriate platform due to its user friendliness, widespread support and various third-party integrations.

The client did not expect high traffic so a shared platform through Bluehost was chosen for its cost efficiency, extensive admin features and easy-to-use zone editing. Once hosted, development began with template design. The client wanted something clean and modern so a classic font was chosen and a simple, dual-tone logo was created using Adobe illustrator.

When it comes to a service-oriented business, providing information to potential customers is the highest priority. Thus, I wanted to make sure that services offered, contact info, and hours were highlighted on the homepage.

The website is still currently under development. Goal completion date is end of August so check back soon for updates!
