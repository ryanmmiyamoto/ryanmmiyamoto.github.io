What do I do for fun?


I like to think of myself as a pretty adventurous person. When I'm not pounding out inspiring and artfully crafted code, you can find me in the middle of the Mojave desert, on the slopes of Mammoth Mountain, or floating around in one of hundreds of lakes dotting the Sierra Nevada. If I'm not there you might catch me weaving through trees in the Santa Monica Mountains, covered in grease and grime under my beloved Subaru, or shanking golf balls at Westchester Golf Course. I'm always down to try new things, experience new scenery or teach people how to do the things that I do. Just be sure to bring a peanut butter stout with you!
