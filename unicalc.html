Overview


Unicalc was a pair programming project I did during my undergraduate years at UCSD. It is a unit conversion calculator that can perform basic arithemtic along with converting a quantified unit of measurement to any other unit in the database. For example, you could convert 25 meters to feet or 190 miles per hour to meters per second. Additionally, for your own entertainment, you could create your own unit of measurement and set it equal to any other quantified unit of measurement. As of right now, Unicalc is a strictly command line tool for the terminal. There are currently no plans to optimize or develop it for iOS or Android platforms.
